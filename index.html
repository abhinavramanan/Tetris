How to Play Tetris

  • Move and rotate falling blocks to complete horizontal lines.
  • Completed lines disappear and score points.
  • Game speed increases as you level up.
  • Use arrow keys to control blocks.
  • Press Esc to pause. Click Continue to advance to the next level.
  • Game ends when the stack reaches the top.
